💡 Why I Built This

Most small businesses track their leads in WhatsApp chats, sticky notes, or messy Excel sheets. Things fall through the cracks. Follow-ups get missed. Deals are lost.

I built LeadFlow CRM to solve exactly that — a clean, simple dashboard where a small sales team can see every lead, track where each one stands, and actually close more deals.

No subscription fees. No complex setup. Just open the browser and start working.


🖥️ What It Looks Like

The project has two parts:

1. The Public Website — What your customers see when they visit. A professional landing page that explains your product, shows pricing, and lets visitors book a demo or contact you.

2. The Admin Panel — What you (the business owner or sales team) use every day. A private dashboard to manage everything behind the scenes.


✨ What You Can Do With It

As a Visitor (Public Site)


Read about the product and its features
Compare pricing plans (Starter / Professional / Enterprise)
Book a demo or send a message
Subscribe to the newsletter
Browse the FAQ


As an Admin (Admin Panel)


See your numbers instantly — Total leads, qualified leads, conversion rate, and monthly revenue all on one screen
Add leads in seconds — Name, email, company, phone, deal value, notes — done
Track where every lead stands — New → Contacted → Qualified → Converted
Search instantly — Find any lead by name, email, or company
Manage your team — Add sales reps, track who's handling how many leads
Check your pipeline — See where leads are dropping off in your funnel

🚀 How to Run It

No installations. No npm install. Nothing complicated.

Quick way — just open it:
Double-click index.html. Done.

Better way — run a local server:

bashcd LeadFlow-CRM
python -m http.server 8000

Then visit:


🌐 Website: http://localhost:8000/index.html
🔧 Admin Panel: http://localhost:8000/admin.html
